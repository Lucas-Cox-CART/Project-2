Examples

Examples of Yellow Journalism

Spanish American War

The very first time Yellow Journalism was used was to maliciously push for a war with Spain over events in Cuba, notably the mysterious explosion of the USS Maine that killed two-hundred sixty soldiers.

Ebola is Coming

In 2014, there was an outbreak of Ebola in Western Africa, Bloomberg Businessweek's cover showed in giant font, “Ebola is Coming” in an appearance similar to blood. This overexaggerated what was a serious threat and could have caused panic to some readers.

Baby Snatched by Eagle

This shocking headline grabbed many viewers' attention, but the accompanying video was validated as a fake.
